WISE High School Mentorship 2019-2020 Application

WISE aims to provide undergraduate students the opportunity to develop their leadership skills by giving them the opportunity to reach out to the GTA community to encourage youths to pursue STEM fields. Once you commit to joining the WISE team, you become part of a supportive and innovative family of hard-working individuals with whom you will likely form ever-lasting friendships.

As a mentor, you will be able to help high school students with their professional and personal development, while introducing them to new people, ideas, and inspirations. You will be able to share your university experience to make a positive impact in the lives of your mentees. All students will participate in hackathons, workshops and social events throughout the school year that will help them better formulate their goals, think about their plans beyond high school, and be equipped to face challenges they may face. The following form will help us get to know you better and pair you up with suitable mentees.

Applications will be due on August 23, 2019 at 11:59pm.

Role Requirements

  • Effective listening, communication and leadership skills
  • Time commitment of 4-5 hours per month
  • Team player with positive attitude
  • Commitment to WISE mission

Expectations

  • Attend Mentor training & Design Challenge
  • Participate in all Mentorship events (approximately 3 per semester)
  • Build lasting relationships with mentees & check in with them regularly
  • Assist WISE Outreach team with larger-scale events when needed

Questions or Concerns
