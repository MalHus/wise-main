WISE High School Design Challenge 2019

Calling all high school students! You are invited to apply for the Women In Science and Engineering Design Challenge! The Design Challenge is an exciting event where high school students across the GTA work in teams competing to solve real-world problems. Students will be exposed to existing problems in the sciences and engineering and will allow for the exploration and development of critical thinking and creativity. Participants will simulate the strategic thinking that real-world problems require and present their innovative solutions to a panel of judges. They will also have the opportunity to meet and network with current undergraduate students in STEM related programs!

The WISE Design Challenge application is open to all high school students regardless of grade, gender or future aspirations! The challenge will be held at the University of Toronto St. George Campus on October 5, 2019 from 11:00am-3:00pm. Join us for a fun and engaging afternoon!

Applications to participate will be due on September 21, 2019 at 11:59pm.
