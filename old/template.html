WISE Executive Recruitment: Fall 2019

Women in Science and Engineering (WISE) is a long established organization on the U of T campus, aiming to support and empower women to achieve their full potential as engineers, scientists, and leaders. We are currently recruiting one Graphic Design Director and two Photography and Media Directors to join us for the 2019/2020 school year!

Applications will be due on September 20, 2019 at 11:59pm.

Graphic Design Director Role Description

  • Design posters, banners, and logos for various initiatives
  • Submit promotional materials (i.e. for ECF screens, computers, and slide decks) to VP Marketing
  • Create storyboards and graphics for the website in collaboration with the web developer
  • Estimated Hours: 3-8/week

Photography and Media Director Role Description

  • Responsible for taking photos at WISE events
  • Collaborate with the Stories of WISE editors for headshots
  • Edit photos and upload onto Google Drive for marketing team to post on different social media outlets
  • Assist initiatives with larger-scale events when needed

Questions or Concerns

Please email vp.marketing@wiseuoft.org
