WISE Industry Mentorship Mentee Recruitment

What do famous business leaders, top of the field researchers, and innovative startup founders have in common? They recognize the power of mentorship! Studies show that including mentors in your personal and professional development lead to greater career success. WISE is here to help break down barriers to finding suitable mentors.

We're currently recruiting mentees for our Industry Mentorship program. You will be paired with a mentor in a field of your interest and can use them as a resource for anything you need in terms of finding a job, figuring out what you'd like to do for a career, and so much more!

Applications will be due on September 30, 2019 at 11:59pm.
