WISE Conference 2020 Award for Youth Leaders

The Women in Science and Engineering (WISE) Conference Award for Youth Leaders is an award for the annual WISE National Conference â€“ Lead without Limits - that will bring together delegates from across Canada to share ideas and become inspired over the course of a two-day event dedicated to professional and personal growth. We hope this award will encourage young women to pursue their studiesand careers in STEM and expand their horizons!

Grade 11 and 12 students (both female and male) who have a strong academic background and are currently enrolled in high schools in Ontario are eligible to apply. Priority will be given to students who are leaders in their local STEM community and spend time volunteering or mentoring others in STEM related areas.

All applicants are kindly advised to keep their schedules open for the weekend of the conference (January 25 to 26, 2020).

Learn about the conference: https://conference.wiseuoft.org/

Applications due: January 7, 2020
Winners will be announced: January 11, 2020

Award Details

The WISE Conference Award for Youth Leaders is valued at $250, and includes travel costs, conference tickets announced accommodations for the duration of the conference.

There will be two (2) awards available.
