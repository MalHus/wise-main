WISE HS Mentee Application 2020-2021

Are you interested in Science and Engineering? Interested in learning about what it's like to be a university student? Apply to be a mentee for the WISE High School Mentorship Program! Students who join the High School Mentorship Program are paired with a university student in their STEM field of interest. They will have the opportunity to take part in monthly events aimed at learning more about STEM fields and/or post-secondary life. These events will provide ample opportunities for personal and professional development.

WISE HS Mentor Recruitment 2020-2021

WISE is presenting undergraduate and graduate students with an exciting opportunity to develop their leadership and mentorship skills! Through the WISE High School Mentorship Program, mentors will be able to engage with high school students in the GTA and encourage them to pursue STEM based fields. Once you commit to joining the WISE team, not only do you get to create a positive impact on the lives of students, but you also become a part of our supportive and innovative team of individuals with whom you will likely form lasting connections.

WISE Outreach Ambassador Recruitment 2020-2021

WISE aims to provide undergraduate students the opportunity to develop their leadership skills by giving them the opportunity to reach out to the GTA community to encourage youths to pursue STEM fields.
