Opportunities

Sanofi is hosting the second of a three part development workshop series for the Fall 2023 season, NA Sanofi Workshop Series: Top Tips & Tricks to Showcase Your Expertise in a Job Interview!

About the Workshop: Interviews can sometimes feel so intimidating, but the good news is that they don’t have to be! Join the development workshop session and learn some quick tips and tricks with how to showcase your experiences in a meaningful way. You will also have the opportunity to hear from Sanofi Early Career hires on their interview experiences and also from a recruiter’s perspective on the key things to do and what not to not do.

About Sanofi: We are an innovative global healthcare company, driven by one purpose: we chase the miracles of science to improve people’s lives. Our teams across the world strive to transform the practice of medicine, turning the impossible into the possible for patients. We provide potentially life-changing treatments and the protection of life-saving vaccines to millions of people, and affordable access to our medicines in some of the world’s poorest countries.
