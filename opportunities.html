  • Sales Engineer Summer Intern, Eastern Canada (Toronto): Trane’s summer internship allows participants to experience a program focused on exploring a career in Technical Sales. Our commercial business systems interns work with Industry leading Sales Professionals and our customers in the design, application and sale of systems, controls, parts and services for commercial, industrial and institutional buildings. This includes the world's most complete line of commercial, industrial and institutional heating, ventilating and air conditioning (HVAC) equipment and building management controls. Throughout your internship at one of our nationwide sales offices, you will be exposed to the many facets of Trane’s businesses: Service, Equipment, Building Automation, Application, Fulfillment and much more. You will attend sales meetings; business functions; sales and service calls; customer meetings alongside experienced account managers and mentors. You will understand the steps sales engineers take from start to finish on a job and will be hands-on in those steps throughout the internship. In addition, you will gain an appreciation for the importance of the relationship with our customers as you work with them on a daily basis as a point-of-contact creating a sense of ownership and importance in the solution-making process. Our structured twelve-week (12) program will challenge and develop your technical, business, sales, leadership and communication skills. It will give you insight into sales engineering career with Trane and create the opportunity to join our industry leading Graduate Training Program (GTP) upon graduation for those who mutually agree to pursue a career in Technical Sales.

Hi, We’re Sensibill.

Want to join a team that makes an impact? We know that efficient receipt management is critical to the health of any business. Since 2013, the Sensibill team has been committed to building innovative AI and ML-powered tools that help banks and technology companies turn everyday spend data into actionable insights. Banks use our digital solutions (like receipt capture and management) to provide users with a way to track receipts for returns, exchanges, expenses, accounting, and taxes. Our​ mission is simple: build better digital

banking tools that drive engagement and insights so financial institutions can nurture financially healthy customers.

Our products reach millions of users worldwide, and you have the opportunity to make a difference. Everyone’s voice matters and everyone plays a role in building a thriving, socially-responsible technology company.

A Day In The Life

As a member of our Machine Learning team, you will:

●Work with on our Data Platform: either driving the performance of our processing engine or building insights out of data;

●Discover and propose new models that will help us deliver delightful experiences to our end users;

●Work towards the productization of our different solutions.

A little bit about you:

●Working towards a degree in engineering, statistics, computer science or related field

●Experienced in Python;

●Have an understanding of deep learning frameworks, such as PyTorch or TensorFlow;

●Proficiency in data structures, algorithms, and software design;

●Excellent communication skills.

Are You The Next Sensibillian To Join Our Team?

Ready to join a dedicated team and make a truly positive impact through your work as part of a thriving Toronto startup making waves in the world of fintech? You may just be our next Sensibillian!

Suppose you want to solve unique problems to improve the lives of those we serve and want to contribute your skills, passion, integrity, and commitment to excellence to a growing startup with a rewarding and inclusive culture. In that case, we’d love to hear from you!

Though our headquarters are located in Toronto, Ontario, you will be working remotely in a full-time capacity. At Sensibill, our teams use a range of virtual collaborative work and communication tools, along with digital training and onboarding resources, so you’ll always feel connected to your teammates and our ​organization at large!

When you intern at Sensibill, you have the opportunity to:

●Work with the latest technologies and tools while honing your skills and passions

●Contribute to our work and mission in building modern digital banking tools

●Learn from industry experts and thought leaders across specialities like Machine Learning, Data & AI, Engineering and more

●Gain hands-on experience with real-world business objectives in a fast-paced, forward-thinking fintech startup

●Kick-start your career!

Diversity And Inclusion

To us, they’re not buzzwords. They’re the lifeforce behind Sensibill.

At Sensibill, the differences that make each of us who we are, the perspectives that help define how we think and work, and our individually unique experiences help us drive innovation, both in and outside of the company. We don’t just value diversity but believe it’s the lifeforce behind our work here at Sensibill. By working alongside teammates with different backgrounds and perspectives, we can create a platform for change and real impact. To do that, we build teams that reflect the very real people we serve and where everyone feels empowered to bring their whole and authentic selves to work. We strongly encourage all qualified candidates to apply, regardless of race, religion, gender, sexual orientation, age, origin, or disability.
